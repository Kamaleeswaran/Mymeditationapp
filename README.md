# Mymeditationapp
The mindfull meditaion timer app that saves your meditaion journey and also a streak based gamified themed app
Meditation Timer App
Welcome to your personal space for mindfulness. This Meditation Timer App is designed to help you build a consistent habit, track your progress, and find peace through a beautiful, glowing, and immersive experience.

🌟 Key Features
Glowing Theme: Experience a calming, ambient interface that utilizes soft, luminous gradients and subtle animations to help you relax the moment you open the app.

Persistent Streak Tracking: Never lose motivation. The app automatically tracks your consecutive days of meditation, encouraging you to show up for yourself every single day.

Customizable Timers: Whether you have one minute or an hour, adjust your session length to fit perfectly into your schedule.

Firebase Integration: Securely save your progress and sync your streaks across devices using Google Authentication.

🎯 Use Cases
Morning Mindfulness: Start your day with a clear mind by setting a short, 5-minute session upon waking.

Mid-Day Reset: Use the timer during a lunch break to reduce stress and boost productivity.

Building a Habit: Use the Streak Tracker to gamify your practice and build the discipline required to make meditation a lifelong habit.

Evening Wind-down: Utilize the glowing, low-light aesthetic to prepare your mind for a restful night of sleep.

Find your calm. Build your streak.!
